About MealBridge

MealBridge is a smart food donation platform designed to reduce food waste and support communities in need. The platform connects donors, volunteers, and food seekers through a simple and modern web application.

Our mission is to ensure that surplus food reaches the right people instead of being wasted. MealBridge encourages kindness, community participation, and social responsibility by helping create a better and hunger-free society. MealBridge focuses on creating social impact through technology and community support.
